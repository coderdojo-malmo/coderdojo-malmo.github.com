Come learn or teach to code, hack, make web sites and apps.

CoderDojo is an Irish led, global movement of coding clubs for young people.

Our CoderDojo will focus to learn 7-17 years old how to make web sites, apps, etc.

We have just initiated CoderDojo Malmö and if you are interested to participate as mentor, parent to participating kids or if you would like to attend, please send up to our Google Group here. You can also follow our movements on Twitter or Facebook.

Please note that if you are under 12 years old - you need to attend together with a parent.

Our first CoderDojo Malmö takes place April 11, 17.00-19.00 at Media Evolution City, Stora Varvsgatan 6a, Malmö (map).
